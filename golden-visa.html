Benefits of a Spanish
Golden Visa

✓ High quality of life

✓ Ability to include a spouse or unmarried partner, financially dependent children of any age (adult children must be full-time students), and financially dependent parents of the main applicant and/or their spouse who are 65 years and older

✓ Access to the public healthcare and education systems in the country

✓ A vibrant EU country that has majestic landscapes

✓ The possibility to apply for Spanish citizenship after two years of effective residence for Sephardic Jews and their descendants, and for citizens of Ibero-American countries, Andorra, Equatorial Guinea, Portugal, and the Philippines

✓ Excellent schools, with tuition available in English, French, German, Italian, Japanese, and Mandarin

How can I get a
Golden Visa?

✓ Through the purchase of Real Estate for a value of more than €500,000. But it is important to take into account that, if several investors participate in the same real estate, the investment will be computed at an individual level. This means that if our personal contribution to the real estate in question does not reach 500,000€, we will not be able to apply for the visa.

✓ To own shares or participations in a Spanish company.

✓ To have a bank deposit in a Spanish entity with more than one million euros.

✓ To have possession of Spanish public debt for a value of 2 million euros or more.

✓ Actively participate in a business project to be developed in Spain. It is essential that the project generates employment, and promotes scientific and technological progress in the country, or that it has a minimum socioeconomic impact.
